What is BEST?

Boosting Engineering Science and Technology

Origin

The BEST competition was started in 1993 by two Texas Instruments engineers. The BEST competition included only 14 schools and 221 students in its first year, but now is comprised of over six hundred schools and eleven thousand students across the nation.

Game

Each year, a new game is created by the BEST organization. Every team is given a "kit" of wood, PVC pipe, and other miscellaneous items to build their robot. Students will get hands on experience in many skills, such as using tools to build the robot, writing a notebook, making 3D models in CAD, and much more.

Career Path

The competition is designed to encourage careers in the fields of engineering and science, but regardless of what career a student may pursue, the BEST competition will develop teamwork and leadership skills that will be beneficial in all walks of life.

This Year's Competition
